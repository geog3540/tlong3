Geo Visualization Portfolio GEO:3540

Percent Bachelors Degree in Michigan

The county level Michigan data shows largest concentration of bachelors degrees around the same area in the east part of the state where the University of Michigan is located. The rest of the counties show relatively low amounts of bachelor degrees.

Green House Gas Emissions

In this proportional symbols map, there is a large amount of GHG emissions all across the United States. The most appear to be around highly populated areas and large cities on the east coast.

Unemployed vs Disabilities in Los Angeles

This map looks at the census level in Los Angeles of percent unemployed vs percent with disabilities. There is little to no correlation between the two variables. there are not many areas where there is both high unemployment and high percent disabilites.

Divvy Bike Flow in Chicago

There is a high amount of bike flow within chicago. The main bike flow appears to occur close to the lake. There is less bike activity getting further into the city and away from the coastline. The points represent end destinations. High values of end destination are correlated with high amounts of bike flow.

Yearly Total Precipitation in Iowa

Isarithmic mapping was used to map out yearly precipitation in Iowa. We find the highest precipitation happening in northeast Iowa whereas the lowest precipitation occurs in the southwest.

Disease spread among Bur Oak Trees Project

In this project disease spread was tracked among bur oak trees in the Iowa Great Lakes area. We used vector data to visualize trees being effected. We distributed the data into four sections around the Iowa Great Lakes: Gull Point, Hales Slough, Kenue Park, and Lakeside Lab. From here we identified spread, recovery, and potential causes of disease spread.
