Percent Bachelors Degree in Michigan

At the county level within the state of Michigan we see the largest concentration of bachelors degree around the same area in the east part of the state. This is likely where Michigan State University is located and larger cities. The rest of the counties show relatively low amounts of bachelor degrees.

Green House Gas Emissions

A large amount of GHG emissions are found all across the United States. The most appear to be around highly populated areas and large cities on the east coast.

Unemployed vs Disabilities in Los Angeles

This map looks at the census level in Los Angeles of percent unemployed vs percent with disabilities. There overall appears to be no correlation between the two variables. There are very limited areas that see a high percent of both Unemployed and those with Disabilities

Divvy Bike Routes in Chicago

There is a large amount of bike flow close to the lake. There is less bike activity getting further into the city and away from the coastline.
